# 🏙️ Future City Simulator 2040

## Advanced AI-Driven City Building & Management Simulation

### Overview
Future City Simulator 2040 is a cutting-edge, browser-based city simulation game featuring advanced AI advisory systems, dynamic event management, real-time visualization, and comprehensive city metrics tracking.

### 🌟 Features

#### Core Gameplay
- **12 Unique Building Types**: Residential, Commercial, Industrial, Parks, Solar Farms, Hospitals, Schools, Research Labs, Airports, Stadiums, Hydro Plants, Museums
- **6 Key Metrics**: Population, Money, Happiness, Environment, Technology, Energy
- **Policy System**: 6 different city policies with long-term effects
- **Level Progression**: Unlock new capabilities as your city grows

#### AI Systems
- **Intelligent Recommendations**: AI analyzes your city and provides strategic advice
- **Predictive Analytics**: Forecasts potential challenges before they occur
- **Dynamic Insights**: Real-time analysis of city performance and trends
- **Adaptive Messaging**: Context-aware guidance based on current situation

#### Event System
- **Random Events**: 15+ different events including positive, negative, and choice-based scenarios
- **Emergency System**: Handle crises like natural disasters, cyber attacks, and health emergencies
- **Festival System**: Host city events to boost happiness
- **Dynamic Requirements**: Events trigger based on city state and building composition

#### Visualization
- **Real-time 2D Rendering**: Beautiful canvas-based city visualization
- **Dynamic Weather**: Sky changes based on environmental metrics
- **Building Animations**: Floating animations, glowing effects, window lights
- **Interactive Canvas**: Click to place buildings, zoom, and rotate view
- **Particle Effects**: Clouds, flying vehicles, and atmospheric effects

### 🚀 Getting Started

#### Installation
1. Create project folder: `future-city-simulator/`
2. Create subfolders: `css/`, `js/`
3. Copy all files to their respective locations
4. Open `index.html` in a modern web browser

#### No Dependencies Required!
- Pure JavaScript (ES6+)
- No frameworks needed
- No build process required
- Works offline

### 🎮 How to Play

#### Starting Out
1. Open the game in your browser
2. You start with $1,000,000 and 10,000 citizens
3. Review AI recommendations in the right panel
4. Build structures by clicking building icons then clicking the canvas

#### Building Strategy
- **Residential**: Increases population
- **Commercial**: Generates steady income
- **Industrial**: High income but harms environment
- **Parks**: Boosts happiness and environment
- **Solar/Hydro**: Provides clean energy
- **Hospitals/Schools**: Improves happiness and technology

#### Managing Your City
- **Monitor all 6 metrics** - balance is crucial
- **Follow AI advice** - it analyzes your situation
- **Respond to events** - some require quick decisions
- **Enact policies** - long-term strategic benefits
- **Level up** - population growth unlocks new levels

#### Controls
- **Play/Pause**: Control simulation flow
- **Speed Controls**: 1x, 2x, 4x game speed
- **Save/Load**: Persist your progress
- **Reset**: Start a new city
- **Zoom/Rotate**: Navigate the city view

### 📊 City Metrics Explained

#### Population
- Grows when happiness > 60%
- Shrinks when happiness < 40%
- Increases tax revenue
- Required for unlocking features

#### Money
- Earned from taxes, commercial, and industrial buildings
- Lost to maintenance and policies
- Required for all construction
- Manage carefully to avoid bankruptcy

#### Happiness
- Affected by services (hospitals, parks)
- Decreases with poor conditions
- Critical for population growth
- Triggers events when too low

#### Environment
- Damaged by industrial buildings
- Improved by parks and green energy
- Affects temperature and air quality
- Environmental crises occur when too low

#### Technology
- Increased by labs, schools, and policies
- Unlocks better buildings and options
- Attracts investments
- Enables research breakthroughs

#### Energy
- Consumed by buildings and population
- Generated by solar and hydro plants
- Blackouts occur when depleted
- Critical for city operations

### 🤖 AI Advisory System

The AI continuously monitors your city and provides:

1. **Priority Recommendations**: Urgent issues first
2. **Strategic Advice**: Long-term planning guidance
3. **Performance Insights**: Trend analysis and feedback
4. **Predictive Warnings**: Forecasts of upcoming challenges
5. **Optimal Building Suggestions**: Best next structure to build

### 🎲 Event System

#### Event Types
- **Positive**: Breakthroughs, booms, investments
- **Negative**: Outages, pollution, protests
- **Choice-Based**: Decisions that affect outcome
- **Emergencies**: Major crises requiring response

#### Event Triggers
Events can trigger based on:
- Time intervals (every 45 seconds)
- City metrics (happiness, environment, etc.)
- Building presence (labs, airports, etc.)
- Random probability

### 💾 Save System

- **Auto-save**: Happens on Save button click
- **Local Storage**: Saved in browser
- **Persistent**: Survives page refresh
- **Load Anytime**: Restore previous session
